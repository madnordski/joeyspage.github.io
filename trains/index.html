Lionel Train Photos

Lionel trains are, for the most part, toy trains. Unlike model trains, toy trains do not conform to scale. This does not prevent the toy train collector or operator from creating elaborate layouts and scenery.

Below are some photographs from my first attempt at creating a scene for my toy trains. The photographs are simple flash photos, as photography is not one of my hobbies. Click on any of the small photos to see the full size picture.
