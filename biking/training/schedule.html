2002 RTR Training Schedule

DateDescriptionMiles (aprox)Start
March 30 South on Sugar River and Fritz, West on Wittwer & A, N on Primrose Center? to Mt Vernon, home via G and J. A few hills and some nice spring views (25 if we take G to Dairy Ridge on the return). 22 to 25 Dan's
April 6 Same start, but extrend S. to Ridge Drive - West to G. Meander a bit W of G while on way back to Mt. Vernon. We had numerous forced forays into the fields, careening crabapples and chainring-cut calves, and were lucky no one was hurt more seriously. Gusts were 60mph, from Evan's 2001 log. 30 to 35 Dan's
April 13 From Paoli to New Glarus for lunch. A great ride to New Glarus for lunch; the air was what I liked most -- fresh, dry and cool. The views were wonderful and spring was in the air. -- from Pat's 2001 log. 35 Paoli
April 20 To Lodi for lunch from Festge Park. Strong SSW wind in the morning with lots of scud clouds. Our route took us first North to Crystal Lake, then East to Lodi. We were blown most of the way. After a fine lunch, with spring-fevered ducks for entertainment, we took the shortest way back. -- from Dan's 2001 log. 60 Festge Park, Near Cross Plains
April 27 Meet in Sauk City Loop - Joe's Freedom Ride. Perfect temporature, perfect quiet country roads, lots of training climbs, and a wild section on a one lane,paved twisting road. What more can I ask for? -- from Dan's 2001 log. 60 Hwy 12 & Sycamore in Sauk City
May 4 Meet in Mt Horeb for 40 miles of SW Dane hills and ridges. If we aren't in freezing rain, maybe lunch in New Glarus and 50 miles. rainy, windy and cold (40's); group decision to turn back after about the first ten miles; my legs felt like lead today; Dennis got TWO flats; Jack took a fall; all and all the ride could have been a lot more pleasant! -- from Pat's 2001 log. 40 to 50 Mt. Horeb Grundahl Park
May 11 Meet at Jack's house for 40 - 50 miles of Iowa Co. ridges. 50Jack's
May 18 From Dan's to New Glarus for lunch. A few long hills. Had a nice lunch at Deningers, sitting on the porch. It was a good ride, alot of hills. I started to feel comfortable, and actually enjoy, going down the steeper ones. Hope the good feeling lasts. -- from Barb's 2000 log. 65Paoli
May 25 Meet at Jack's house and head to Mineral Point for lunch.  [a] ride from my place with this year's group except the Blanchard's who would have loved it. Dennis and Dan and I split off from Joe, Pat and Barb at the top of Ryan Rd. Our ride on Mounds View, H, Prairie Rd, Clay Hill Rd, W and 39 with a tailwind was to our liking. We arrived at Brewery Creek Brew Pub 20 some min. ahead of the other group... This Pub remains a fantastic place for lunch: my choices of their IPA, green salad with house dressing, salmon burger and black coffee were excellent: my only regret was passing on the cheesecake or other dessert. -- from Jack's 2001 log. 80 to 90Jack's
June 1 If we want another long ride - Meet at Paoli for the triple retro ride. This ride picks up portions of three Wed. night rides in the opposite direction. Late lunch in New Glarus. 80Paoli
