Lunch at the Red Rooster - Pasties were dry, but the Eggs Benedict, pancakes and hash browns were good! Adam developed heat exhaustion (despite 3+ Quarts of fluid - we're not sure he was adequately fueled at that point, however), and Wendy picked us up at MacDonalds in Dodgeville. 58 644.8
