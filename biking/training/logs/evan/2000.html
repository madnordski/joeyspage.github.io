Bicycling Community Page Training Log, 2000
by Joseph King and Scott Rose

Evan & Adam

Last Year's Log

DateDescriptionMiles Total
30 March To Timber Lane and back. Evan fell and scraped knee, flatted front tubular. 9.9 9.9
1 April South on Sugar River and Fritz, West on Wittwer & A, N on Primrose Center to Mt Vernon, home via G and J. 22.3 32.2
5 April Wednesday Night Ride (Paoli Circle). Very windy. 19.8 52
29 April Sauk to North Freedom with Rockies gang. 51.7 103.7
4 May Home to Observatory and Barlow Hills and back via Mineral Pt. Climbed 1200 feet. 24.1 127.8
6 May Mt. Horeb to New Glarus. Lunch on porch at Deininger's - Apple Pancakes! Arrived late to start, but rode into Mt. Horeb precisely at the same time as Jack/Barb and Dennis/Dan! 2100 Ft. climbed. 42 169.8
7 May Home-Blackhawk-Twin Lakes-14-Rocky Dell-Airport-P-Hilltopper-Enchanted Valley-Schneider-Vosen-Rocky Dell-Cleveland-Home. Climbed 1550ft. 29.6 199.4
13 May From Jack's - Blue Mounds Trail, Ryan Rd, Mounds Park, Ridge View, HH, B.G. Rd, T, Oak Rd, Dugway, etc. Climbed 4050ft. Lunch at Spring Green visitors center. High 55, extremely windy, with little sun. Adam flatted front wheel. CO2 adapter pulled valve core out! 56.2 255.6
14 May Bombay Westin Wood Ride. Evan was knocked down by a carelees rider on the Lakeview bike trail, damaging the (Rensho) rear derailleur, fixed 5 miles later, restoring normal shifting. Minor right knee abrasion. Climbed 2310ft. 43.2 298.8
25 May To Cross Plains with detour down/up Cleveland hill. 1000 ft. climbing. 20.2 319
28 May Mineral Point from Jack's with everyone - great meal at the Brewery. Cool and cloudy all day. Just beat the rain. 3870 ft. elevation gained. 77.8 396.8
3 June Paoli to Evansville with the group. Nice partly cloudy weather. Climbed 1470ft. Adam flatted rear wheel. 48 444.8
6 June Wednesday Night Ride - Lodi to Merrimac Ferry loop. Evan was on call, but survived! Beautiful evening. Rode with Dan, while Joe wisely tended to his marraige.16 mph. 1000 ft. gained. 26 470.8
10 June Paoli to New Glarus with Dan & Dennis. Hot, humid and incredibly windy.Shortened the route due to heat and threat of rain. Our final ride, and my lowest training miles since my first ride in '93, despite fairly good weather during training season. While we feel reasonably strong, endurance may be lacking. We expect to have a great time, but may be fairly sore the first few days! 35 505.8

Total Miles: 505.8
