Practice Criterium Photos

These pictures were taken on July 9th, 2002 by Patricia King. The Brazen Dropouts hold these practice criteriums the second Tuesday of the month (May - August) at the Fitchburg Center Business Park. Registration begins at 6 PM.

Note: Older versions of Netscape will distort some of the photos. You can work around this problem by loading the link into a new window. Be patient -- each picture will take a moment or two to load.
