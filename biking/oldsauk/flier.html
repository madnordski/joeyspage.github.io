On October 26th I distributed about 125 of these fliers to homes and codos on Old Sauk Road and inside the Blackhawk Subdivision.

I spoke with six people.

  • Two on Old Sauk Road seemed ammused that anyone was still trying to fight a fight they had already lost for themselves. These people live near enough to Junction Road to have their yards torn up already.

  • It was almost an hour later before I was engaged again. Most of the intervening time was spent leafleting big fancy homes so I was beginning to wonder if I was doing more harm than good. A woman came to see what I had for her and when she did she was eagar to call Sue Hamblin and give her hell!

  • The other 3 people I talked to that live in big fancy homes were also interested in calling Sue to stop this project.

Overall, I think this was worthwhile. I hope that these people don't feel jerked around now that the decision in postponed. Do you think we had something to do with that?
