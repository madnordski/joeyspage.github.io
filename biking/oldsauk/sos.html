S.O.S. - Save Old Sauk Road

 

STOP THE PLAN TO DIRECT BELTLINE TRAFFIC PAST YOUR DOOR

Tomorrow a Madison commission will consider a plan to relieve traffic congestion at Beltline interchanges by directing traffic to the Old Sauk Rd. interchange. The proposal calls for the widening of Old Sauk Road and parts of Pleasant View Road to four lanes.

  • It would INCREASE NOISE pollution by bringing more trucks up Old Sauk Road to Pleasant View Rd.
  • It would INCREASE AIR POLLUTION by bringing traffic bound for Mineral Point Road and US Highway 14 past your front door.
  • Call Alderperson Hamblin NOW and tell her you oppose this plan!
