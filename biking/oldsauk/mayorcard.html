Dear Mayor Bauman,

I think that one of the reasons that Madison is such a wonderful city is that there still exist rural roads that reach in toward the center of the City. Setting aside a few roads and working toward maintaining their rural character won't be easy, but it would be a wise and valued choice for the future.

I urge you to intercede to stop plans to develop Old Sauk Road into a four-lane bypass of the Mineral Point and U.S. 14 interchanges. Please take the lead and stop the destruction of central Madison's last remaining rural connection to the beauty of Wisconsin's driftless area. Let Seminole Highway, with its efficient two lanes accompanied by bike lanes, serve as the model for improvements to Old Sauk Road.

Sincerely,

 

 

Citizen
