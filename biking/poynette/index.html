Pie Ride to Poynette

presented by, Bombay Bicycle Club, August 2, 1998

Memory Lane Restaurant is on Main Street in Poynette. They make their own pie and usually have nice selection. They also serve a full breakfast and lunch.

The route is mostly flat with few long hills. It is also mostly open so be sure to drink plenty of water.

The short route takes you through Dane. There you will find a convenience store that has cold sandwiches. They do not serve pizza on Sunday.

This is the first time these cue sheets are being used so beware of errors. They have been checked over many times for directions but not mileage.

Please send corrections to, Joe King, jking@mailbag.com.
