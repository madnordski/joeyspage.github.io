2001 RTR Training Schedule

DateDescriptionMiles (aprox)Start
March 31 South on Sugar River and Fritz, West on Wittwer & A, N on Primrose Center? to Mt Vernon, home via G and J. A few hills and some nice spring views. 22 - 25 Dan's
Apr. 7 Same start, but extrend S. to Ridge Drive - West to G. Meander a bit W of G while on way back to Mt. Vernon. I enjoy the long views from Ridge Drive, Joe. 35 Dan's
April 14 From Paoli to New Glarus for lunch. A few long hills. A hilly ride to New Glarus with the RTR group. I was more tired afterwards than I think I should have been after only 35 miles, Barb (from last year's log). 35Paoli
Apr 21 To Lodi for lunch from Festge Park. The wind-assisted trip up Lodi-Springfield road was a joy. The trip back required more effort but it afforded some picture postcard views. Besides the Great Blue Heron, Sand Hill Crane, and Mallards we were treated to a flock of American Coots floating on Crystal Lake. Just down the road I spotted a small collection of Wood Ducks paddling in the calm waters of a small pond, Joe (from last year's log). 40 Festge Park, Near Cross Plains
Apr 28 Sauk City to North Freedom for lunch. Some long hills and fantastic views. From Jack's log: From the fertile river valley, up and over the very 'private' Baraboo Range, then out to the west, north and east again where the land is mostly high and open. A wonderful drop into the North Freedom area. The town features a respectable 'Railroad Restaurant.' The return route on Freedom Road was even better, having that private feel from beginning to end. Some areas should never be allowed to change. This would be one of them. 50Sauk City, auto dealership
May 5 Meet in Mt Horeb Grundahl Park for 40 miles of SW Dane hills and ridges (lunch in New Glarus). From Barb's log: Had a nice lunch at Deningers, sitting on the porch. It was a good ride, alot of hills. I started to feel comfortable, and actually enjoy, going down the steeper ones. Hope the good feeling lasts. We climbed about 2700 feet. 40Mt. Horeb
May 12 Meet at Jack's house for 40 - 50 miles of Iowa Co. ridges. 50Jack's
May 19 Blanchardville from my house. Hope there is food there. It was about 65 miles. A beautiful, quiet route both to and from Blanchardville from Dan's. The views from county F were outstanding, Jack. 65Dan's
May 26 Meet at Jack's house. Mineral Point and lunch. From Joe's log: Lunch at the brew pub on Commerce Street was fabulous. I had the soup, walnut burger, and fries. The jalapeno poppers were very well done. The brews were good and the atomosphere of the old stone building was charming. Before and after lunch we biked on the dead quiet roads of Iowa County. The beauty of Mounds View road was complemented by a great blue. It was a cool gray day with a bit of a head wind heading back. 80Jack's
Jun 4 If we want another long ride - Meet at Paoli for the triple retro ride. This ride picks up portions of three Wed. night rides in the opposite direction. Late lunch in New Glarus About 80. Or flat ride to Evansville. About 40 miles. Alternatively, I'll suggest a sixty to eighty miler to Cambridge. During an XC skiing outing this past winter, Pat and I discovered an old mill that had been transformed into a fine restaurant. They serve high quality food at a high price. -- Joe 40 - 80Paoli
June 11 New Glarus for lunch. 42 Paoli
