QUE-SHEET—A Hilly Fifty from Jack’s

 

Description:  Jack’s to Mill Valley Cheese Factory to Barneveld to Mt Horeb to Jack’s; light traffic, fine scenes, exciting descents, challenging climbs

 

 

1.   Right on Old Indian Trail to north end

2.   Right on F

3.   Left on Fessenfelt/Reeve (hill, stay left after steep descent) to end

4.   Right on KK to end

5.   Right on K

6.   Left on Knight Hollow to end (hill over divide between watersheds)

7.   Right on HH to north end

8.   Left on H—very short (active cheese factory)

9.   Left on Blue Ridge to south end (hill followed by superb ridge views)

10.  Right on HH to south end

11.  Right on K

 

NOTE!!  A rest stop is recommended in Barneveld  (25 mile mark--park, bathrooms, water, grocery).  The next 15 miles to Mt Horeb is all up and down—don’t ride it on empty.

 

12.  Continue south on K

13.  Left on Prairie Grove Rd—CAUTION!! sandy, tricky intersect with Mounds View                                                                                                    after first descent—continue to end of PGR (2 hills)

14.  Jog right on F

15.  Left on West Blue Mounds Rd (nice descent)

16.  Jog left on Z

17.  Continue right on West Blue Mounds Rd to end (hill)

18.  Left on E

19.  Right on Fertile Ridge to end (hills)

20.  Right on 78 ½ mile to:

21.  Left on Barton Rd to end—first ½ firm gravel, but paved on final fast descent--

22.  Left on Sand Hill Rd to Mt Horeb—FOOD!!!  (40 mile mark)

23.  From Main street, North on JG (much new pavement after Stewart Park)

24.  Right on Bohn Rd to end

25.  Left on  J to its north end

26.  Right on F

27.  Right on Blue Mounds Trail (2 hills)

28.  Left on Old Indian Trail to Jack’s  (51 mile mark)
